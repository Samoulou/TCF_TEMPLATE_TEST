Tax and inheritance planning

Beaulac Management Services

Carefully elaborated tax and inheritance planning is at the core of our asset management strategy. The purpose of Beaulac Management Services (BMS) is to optimize our clients’ tax burden and structure your inheritance planning so it protects you and your heirs.

Multi-generational and multi-jurisdictional wealth management can be extremely complicated, especially with the increase of internationally-dispersed families. BMS is the professional partner to whom you can entrust all aspects of your wealth management.

Wealth optimization

Incorporation, administration, domiciliation and liquidation of companies, foundations and trusts

Inheritance and tax planning

Management, restructuring, liquidation of assets (financial, real estate, industrial, etc.)

Registration and management of trademarks, patents, licenses and royalties

Insurance and pension planning

Our Services

Our network of experts

Increasingly geographically dispersed families and the rise of international investment exacerbates the complexity of inheritance planning amongst the members of a family. It is therefore necessary to collaborate with the right professional partner in order to structure these operations in the most efficient way, while respecting the governing legal framework.

BMS has developed an international network of world-class tax lawyers that allows us to operate in any jurisdiction, in order to meet the specific needs of each client.
